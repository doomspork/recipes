---
title: "Braised Tenderloin Tips"
servings: "8 Servings"
prep_time: "15 minutes"
cook_time: "2 hours"
total_time: "2h 15m"
method: "Stovetop"
ingredients:
  - amount: "3 lbs"
    name: "beef tenderloin, cut into 2\" pieces"
  - amount: "1 1/2 tsp"
    name: "soy sauce"
  - amount: "1 tsp"
    name: "Worcestershire sauce"
  - amount: "1/2 tsp"
    name: "black pepper, freshly cracked"
  - amount: "1 1/2 tsp"
    name: "kosher salt"
  - amount: "2 tbsp"
    name: "avocado oil"
  - amount: "1 medium"
    name: "yellow onion, diced"
  - amount: "4-4 1/2 cups"
    name: "beef broth"
  - amount: "2 cups"
    name: "water"
  - amount: "2 1/2 tbsp"
    name: "onion soup mix"
  - amount: "1 tbsp"
    name: "tomato paste"
  - amount: "1 tsp"
    name: "browning sauce (optional)"
directions:
  - "In a large bowl, toss beef tenderloin with soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, black pepper, and salt. Make sure each piece is thoroughly coated."
  - "Heat a large braiser or Dutch oven over medium-high. Add avocado oil, then sear beef in batches (don't overcrowd) for 3 minutes per side until browned. Set aside."
  - "Add onion to the pan, deglazing with a splash of broth if needed. Saut√© until softened, about 5-7 minutes."
  - "Push onions aside and add tomato paste. Stir for 1-2 minutes until fragrant and deeper red."
  - "Return beef to pan. Add onion soup mix, 4 cups beef broth, and water. Stir in browning sauce if using. Taste and adjust salt."
  - "Bring to a boil, cover, and reduce heat to medium. Simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally."
  - "Remove lid and continue simmering until meat is tender and begins to fall apart. Sauce should reduce to a thick gravy. Add remaining broth if too dry. Increase heat to medium-high for a final simmer, then serve."
---
