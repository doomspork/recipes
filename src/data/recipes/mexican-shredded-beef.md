---
title: "Mexican Shredded Beef"
servings: "8 Servings"
prep_time: "10 minutes"
cook_time: "1 hour 20 minutes"
total_time: "1h 30m"
method: "Instant Pot"
ingredients:
  - amount: "2 lbs"
    name: "beef stew meat"
  - amount: "1/2 tsp"
    name: "freshly ground black pepper"
  - amount: "1 tbsp"
    name: "chili powder"
  - amount: "1 tsp"
    name: "dried oregano"
  - amount: "2 tsp"
    name: "kosher salt"
  - amount: "2 tbsp"
    name: "cooking oil"
  - amount: "2 tsp"
    name: "dried minced onion"
  - amount: "3/4 cup"
    name: "beef broth (or water)"
  - amount: "1 tsp"
    name: "ground cumin"
  - amount: "1"
    name: "lime, juiced"
  - amount: "1 tsp"
    name: "garlic powder"
  - amount: "1/2 cup"
    name: "chopped cilantro"
directions:
  - "In a large bowl add beef, chili powder, salt, minced onion, ground cumin, garlic powder, oregano and black pepper. Toss to combine."
  - "Press the saut√© button on the Instant Pot and add the cooking oil. When hot, add the seasoned meat and sear until browned on all sides."
  - "Add the broth, cover with lid, seal the pressure valve and cook for 50 minutes at high pressure."
  - "Open the pressure valve to quickly release the pressure. Carefully open the lid and transfer the beef to a large plate using a slotted spoon."
  - "Shred beef with two forks and then return it to the Instant Pot. Add lime juice, chopped cilantro and stir. Serve and enjoy in tacos, burrito bowls and salads."
