---
title: "Homemade Pizza Crust (00 Flour)"
servings: "2 Thin Crust Pizzas"
prep_time: "15 minutes"
rest_time: "2 hours"
cook_time: "5 minutes"
total_time: "2h 20m"
method: "Dough"
ingredients:
  - amount: "2 cups"
    name: "double zero (00) flour"
  - amount: "3/4 cup"
    name: "warm water"
  - amount: "2 tbsp"
    name: "olive oil"
  - amount: "1 1/4 tsp"
    name: "active dry yeast"
  - amount: "1/2 tsp"
    name: "sugar"
  - amount: "1/4 tsp"
    name: "salt"
directions:
  - "Heat 3/4 cup water to 120-130°F (about 50 seconds in microwave). Stir in sugar until dissolved, then add yeast and stir. Wait 10 minutes until mixture bubbles and develops a yeasty aroma."
  - "In a large bowl or stand mixer, combine flour and salt. Add the water-yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix until dough no longer sticks to the sides of the bowl."
  - "Knead dough until smooth and elastic, about 5 minutes in a stand mixer or 50 kneads by hand."
  - "Coat a clean bowl lightly with olive oil. Place dough inside, cover with plastic wrap and a towel. Rest for at least 2 hours (longer is better)."
  - "Preheat oven to 500°F. Divide dough in half for thin crust pizzas. Stretch each ball from the inside out to desired size."
  - "Place on a pan coated with olive oil. Poke a few holes with a fork to prevent air bubbles. Pre-bake at 500°F for 5-7 minutes until crust crisps, then add toppings and finish baking."
